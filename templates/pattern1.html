💖 No-Sew Crochet Heart Tutorial

soft, beginner-friendly plush pattern

Abbreviations:

sc - single crochet

increase - 2 sc in the same stitch

(x) - stitch count for each round

LET'S GET STARTED!

1. First piece

Make a magic ring & chain 1

Round 1: 6 sc

Round 2: 2 sc in every stitch (12)

Round 3: 1 sc in every stitch (12)

- Fasten off

2. Second piece

Repeat the same steps to make another identical piece (do NOT fasten off)

3. Body

Round 4: join both pieces and sc around (24)

Round 5–8: decrease rounds shaping the heart

Round 9: tight decreases to close shape

Round 10: finish and fasten off

AAAAND… WE’RE DONE!!
